# Project Proposals

This folder contains the various project related propsals for various stakeholders to get their attention, motivation, (financial) support and general buy-in. 

Such proposals normally follow the sequence of

1. **Problem Description**: what is the issue we are going to solve, and why? How does it relate to business and parallel running projects, ...

2. **Expected Results** and Business Case when Problem is solved for various stakeholders

3. **Project Overall Plan**: How can we do it (internal, external), Architecture, Infrastructure, Skills, Resources, 

3. **Next Steps**: What are the next concreete steps that need immediate resources (money, expertise, workforce).

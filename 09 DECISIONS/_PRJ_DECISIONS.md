# Summary of Core Decisions taken along the project. 

Rather than looking for decisions all over the project in minutes, logs, mails, etc. we are collecting all Key-Decisions in this dedicated subfolder. 

Decisions listened here are considered to be final and therefore are mandatory to be followed till explicitely revoked (Revoked decisions are not removed but will be marked as "revoked" with the Revoke being a major decision entry on its own and therefore will be listed in this DecisionList as well. 

## Decision List
The [Decision List](DecisionList.md) with a short Summary of all decision taken is a high level Project Managment Doc and will therefore be accessible from the project's [Dashboard](../01%20DASHBOARD/_Project%20Dashboard.md)
